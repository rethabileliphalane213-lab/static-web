Date Invitation App

A full-stack mobile-first application where users receive a playful date invitation, choose a date, time and meal, and submit their response. Built with Express.js, PostgreSQL and email automation.

Background

I wanted to build something different from the usual CRUD applications. Instead of another inventory or to-do app, I designed a fun date invitation that feels interactive from the very first screen.

The project follows a mobile-first approach because it was designed primarily for smartphone users. One of my favourite features is the moving "No" button, which dodges the user's cursor and encourages them to accept the invitation.

Experience

After accepting the invitation, the user selects a preferred date, time and meal. The information is sent to an Express.js server where it is stored securely in a PostgreSQL database.

Once the submission is complete, an email notification is automatically sent to me containing the invitation details. Building this project allowed me to combine responsive design, JavaScript, Express.js, PostgreSQL, REST APIs, CRUD operations and email integration into one complete application.
